Water goes from the ocean and large bodies by a process called evaporation. Where liquid turns into gas mainly by the sun that has solar engery. Also transpiration which is the process of water movement through a plant and then the water in the plant gets evaporation from the plants leaves and other arieal parts of a plant. The process or condensation turns excess water vapors to cloud droplets mainly when warm air rises and its cools and also losses its capacity to hold water vapors.

Precipitation which is rain, drizzle and others similar to that occurs when

atmosphere becomes saturated with water vapour and falls under gravity. And runoff takes the water back to the ocean and other large bodies areas of water where the cycle starts again.
