What?

The WJ paint watching club is commited to teach the art of paint watching and how to master the art of paint watching to people who are willing to sacrifice countless hours of their life to paint watching.

Who?

Anyone that likes to watch paint.

When?

We meet every monday after school.

Where?

Outside.

Why?

To watch one of the greatest things life can give to us and to appreciate paint drying.
